# Protocol Adoption

Comprehensive analytics for understanding and growing your protocol's adoption.

## What You Can Track

- **Total Value Locked (TVL)** - Real-time and historical TVL across chains
- **User Growth** - Daily/weekly/monthly active users and retention
- **Transaction Volume** - Volume trends and transaction patterns
- **Competitive Analysis** - Market share vs. competitors
