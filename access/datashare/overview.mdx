# Datashare

Get Dune's blockchain data delivered directly to your Snowflake, BigQuery, or Databricks warehouse.

## Why Datashare?

- Query with your existing tools and workflows
- No data egress from your warehouse
- Join with your internal data
- Full SQL access without API limits
