Dune supports SSO controls that help you restrict who can join your workspace and how members authenticate.

## Available controls

### Okta SSO (OIDC)
Use Okta SSO to require members to sign in with Okta. This includes:
- Verifying one or more email domains via DNS
- Configuring an Okta OIDC application
- Enforcing Okta sign-in for workspace members
