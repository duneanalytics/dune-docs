## Why Datashare

- **Privacy**: Query in your warehouse
- **Join your data**: Combine with your existing datasets
- **Control costs**: Manage your own compute
- **Complete dataset**: All the data that powers Dune

## Sharing Identifiers

Each platform uses different methods to access shared data:

- **Snowflake**: Uses share identifiers via Snowflake Marketplace
- **BigQuery**: Uses dataset sharing via Analytics Hub or direct sharing

See platform-specific guides for complete setup instructions.
