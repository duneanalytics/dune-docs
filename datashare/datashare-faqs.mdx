<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I get started?">
    **Snowflake**: Go to [Snowflake Marketplace](https://app.snowflake.com/marketplace/providers/GZSVZMYSCO/Dune) for 30-day trial
    
    **BigQuery**: Email datashares-sales@dune.com for trial setup
    
    **Enterprise**: Visit [dune.com/enterprise](https://dune.com/enterprise)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What platforms do you support?">
    - **Snowflake** (marketplace, worldwide regions)
    - **BigQuery** (custom setup, US Central 1 & EU West 2)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How fresh is the data?">
    24 hours by default. Custom refresh rates available for enterprise.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is it the same data as Dune's platform?">
    Yes, identical data. Data types optimized for each platform (e.g., BigQuery's BIGNUMERIC vs Snowflake's VARBINARY).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I join with my existing data?">
    Yes - query in your own warehouse and join with your datasets.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="[Snowflake] Get button didn't work?">
    You may need to click "Get" twice. Snowflake sometimes needs to prep data first.
  </Accordion>

    - Discord: [#datashare channel](https://discord.gg/2VCqwzzF7u)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
