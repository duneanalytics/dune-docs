# Hyperliquid

[Hyperliquid](https://hyperliquid.xyz) is a high-performance decentralized perpetual futures exchange built on a custom L1 blockchain.

Hyperliquid is a decentralized exchange designed for perpetual futures trading with deep liquidity and low latency. The platform operates on its own Layer 1 blockchain, optimized for trading performance and features an on-chain order book with sub-second block times.
