# Lens Protocol
[Lens Protocol](https://lens.xyz) - a composable and decentralized social graph ready for new connections.

Lens is a decentralized social graph that allows users to own their data and control their social connections. The protocol is designed to be composable, allowing developers to build new social applications on top of the Lens network.

**Website** : [https://lens.xyz](https://lens.xyz)

**Documentation** : [https://docs.lens.xyz](https://docs.lens.xyz)

**Twitter** : [https://twitter.com/lensprotocol](https://twitter.com/lensprotocol)
