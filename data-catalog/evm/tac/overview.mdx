## What is TAC?

TAC (The Asset Chain) is a Layer 2 blockchain built on Ethereum that provides a secure, scalable, and efficient platform for real-world asset tokenization and trading. It enables the seamless integration of traditional financial assets into the blockchain ecosystem, offering institutional-grade infrastructure for asset management and trading.

## Key Features

- **Real-World Asset Focus**: Specialized infrastructure for tokenizing and trading real-world assets
- **Ethereum L2**: Built on Ethereum for security and composability
- **Institutional Grade**: Designed to meet regulatory and institutional requirements
- **High Performance**: Optimized for high-throughput trading and asset management
- **Composability**: Seamless integration with the broader Ethereum ecosystem

## Ecosystem Highlights

- Advanced infrastructure for real-world asset tokenization
- Institutional-grade trading and settlement capabilities
- Regulatory-compliant framework for asset management
- Support for various asset classes including:
  - Real estate
  - Commodities
  - Private equity
  - Fixed income instruments
  - Alternative investments
