Sonic is an EVM layer-1 blockchain platform that offers developers attractive incentives and powerful infrastructure, powering the next generation of decentralized applications. The platform features exceptional performance metrics including 10,000 transactions per second and sub-second finality while maintaining full EVM compatibility.

**High Scalability and Efficiency:**
Sonic delivers exceptional performance with:
- 10,000 Transactions per Second
- Sub-Second Finality
- Low Transaction Costs
- Full EVM Compatibility

**Strong Security and Ethereum Compatibility:**
Sonic maintains robust security through its aBFT (asynchronous Byzantine Fault Tolerance) consensus while offering full EVM compatibility. This allows developers to easily port their Ethereum applications to Sonic or build new ones using familiar tools and languages.

**Developer-Friendly Environment:**
Sonic provides developers with powerful incentives and infrastructure including:
- Fee Monetization: Earn up to 90% of the fees your apps generate
- Innovator Fund: Up to 200 million $S to support new ventures
- Solidity/Vyper Support
- Comprehensive development tooling
