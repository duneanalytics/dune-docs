## Table description
Tracks operational details of transactions in ledgers/blocks. 

Operations are objects that represent a desired change to the ledger: payments, offers to exchange currency, changes made to account options, etc. Operations are submitted to the Stellar network grouped in a Transaction. Each of Stellar's operations has a unique operation object.
